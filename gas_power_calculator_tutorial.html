Tutorial

To investigate your genetic association study design, use the sliders and text boxes in the inputs section to select your parameters.
Given your inputs, the calculted statistical power, expected disease allele frequencies, and probability of disease will be displayed in the results section.
Change the independent variable at the top of the graphs section to visualize the change in power over the range of the selected parameter. All the other parameters remain as are indicated in the inputs section.
